Explore Powai

Modern tech hub with lakeside views and premium lifestyle

About Powai

Powai is one of Mumbai's fascinating neighborhoods with its own unique character and charm. Visitors can experience the local culture, explore markets, and enjoy authentic cuisine.

Exploring the vibrant streets of Powai

What to See

Powai Lake

Enjoy a peaceful sunset by the lake with scenic views. Perfect for evening walks and photography.

IIT Bombay Campus

Explore the lush green campus of one of India's premier engineering institutes. The campus offers beautiful walking paths and architecture.

Hiranandani Gardens

A well-planned township with unique architecture, shopping centers, and beautifully landscaped gardens.

Where to Eat

Mirchi & Mime

An amazing dine-in experience with hearing and speech-impaired staff. They serve delicious Indian cuisine with a modern twist.

Chili's

A solid choice for Tex-Mex cuisine, offering a good selection of American and Mexican dishes in a casual dining atmosphere.

The Terrace

Rooftop dining with lake views, serving a variety of cuisines including North Indian, Chinese, and Continental dishes.
