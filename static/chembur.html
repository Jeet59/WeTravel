Residential neighborhood known for incredible street food

About Chembur

Chembur is one of Mumbai's fascinating neighborhoods with its own unique character and charm. Visitors can experience the local culture, explore markets, and enjoy authentic cuisine.

Exploring the vibrant streets of Chembur

What to See

Diamond Garden

A spacious park perfect for evening strolls and morning jogs, with well-maintained walking paths and green spaces.

Jama Masjid Road

A vibrant road with shops and eateries, ideal for experiencing the local culture and street life in Chembur.

Fine Arts Cultural Centre

A venue hosting various cultural performances, exhibitions, and events throughout the year.

Where to Eat

Grand Central

A stylish venue offering good food and drinks in a relaxed atmosphere, popular for both casual dining and special occasions.

The Bar Stock Exchange

A unique concept bar where drink prices fluctuate based on demand, also serving delicious food options to complement your drinks.

Bhagwati Pav Bhaji

A legendary street-food joint known for its delicious pav bhaji and other Mumbai street food staples.
