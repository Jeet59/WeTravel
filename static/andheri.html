Explore Andheri

Mumbai's entertainment and business hub with film studios, shopping malls, and vibrant nightlife

About Andheri

Andheri is one of Mumbai's fascinating neighborhoods with its own unique character and charm. Visitors can experience the local culture, explore markets, and enjoy authentic cuisine.

Exploring the vibrant streets of Andheri

What to See

Versova Beach

A quieter alternative to Juhu Beach, offering a more peaceful coastal experience with fewer crowds and beautiful sunset views.

Gilbert Hill

A unique 66-million-year-old monolithic rock column, offering panoramic views of the western suburbs from its summit.

Lokhandwala Complex

A vibrant shopping and entertainment district known for its boutiques, cafes, and frequent celebrity sightings.
