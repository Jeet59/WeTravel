Explore Goregaon

Entertainment district with film city and nature parks

About Goregaon

Goregaon is one of Mumbai's fascinating neighborhoods with its own unique character and charm. Visitors can experience the local culture, explore markets, and enjoy authentic cuisine.

Exploring the vibrant streets of Goregaon

What to See

Film City

The iconic Bollywood film production center where many famous movies have been shot. Note: Visitors need permission to enter.

Aarey Forest

A rare green escape in Mumbai, offering walking trails and a glimpse into the city's ecological diversity.

Oberoi Mall

A premium shopping destination with a wide range of international and domestic brands, along with entertainment options.

Where to Eat

The Finch

A popular gastro-pub offering live music with craft beer and a variety of cuisines in a vibrant atmosphere.

Sai Veg World

A local favorite for budget-friendly vegetarian meals, known for its authentic Indian flavors and quick service.

CinCin

An upscale Italian restaurant known for its wood-fired pizzas, handmade pasta, and extensive wine selection.

Local Tips

  • Mind the monkeys in Aarey - avoid carrying visible food items
  • Goregaon East and West are very different - be specific when navigating
  • Hub Mall is good for budget shopping with local brands
  • The Western Express Highway tends to get jammed during rush hours
  • Use the local train to bypass traffic if traveling to South Mumbai
