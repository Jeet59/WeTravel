Explore Lower Parel

Modern commercial hub with upscale malls and restaurants

About Lower Parel

Lower Parel is one of Mumbai's fascinating neighborhoods with its own unique character and charm. Visitors can experience the local culture, explore markets, and enjoy authentic cuisine.

Exploring the vibrant streets of Lower Parel

What to See

High Street Phoenix

A premier shopping destination with international brands, entertainment, and dining options in a stylish mall complex.

Gandhi Museum

A peaceful historical museum offering a dose of calm and history amid the bustling commercial district.

Todi Mills Compound

A revitalized industrial space now hosting trendy restaurants, bars, and creative businesses in a unique setting.

Where to Eat

Toit

A popular microbrewery serving craft beers paired with delicious food in a vibrant, energetic atmosphere.

Tamasha

A trendy restaurant and bar with creative cocktails, diverse menu options, and a lively ambiance perfect for socializing.

Local Tips

  • The area gets super crowded on weekends - visit on weekdays if possible
  • Pre-book cabs out of Kamala Mills post-dinner or you'll be stranded
  • There are hidden speakeasy bars worth discovering for cocktail enthusiasts
  • The area transforms completely from day (business) to night (entertainment)
  • Many restaurant kitchens close earlier than their bar service
