Explore Santacruz

Upscale residential area with parks and shopping options

About Santacruz

Santacruz is one of Mumbai's fascinating neighborhoods with its own unique character and charm. Visitors can experience the local culture, explore markets, and enjoy authentic cuisine.

Exploring the vibrant streets of Santacruz

What to See

Juhu Beach

A short distance from Santacruz, this popular beach offers beautiful sunset views and a variety of street food options.

Air India Colony

A peaceful residential area perfect for walks, with well-maintained gardens and a glimpse into suburban Mumbai living.

Linking Road Shopping

A shopper's paradise with a mix of street stalls and upscale retail outlets, offering fashion, accessories, and more.

Where to Eat

Fatty Bao

A trendy restaurant serving delicious Asian fusion cuisine, known for its creative baos, small plates, and crafted cocktails.

Raju Sandwichwala

An iconic street food stall outside NM College, famous for its unique Mumbai-style sandwiches that are a must-try for visitors.

Local Tips

  • Western Express Highway jams are a pain - consider alternative routes during peak hours
  • Auto rickshaws are usually better than cabs in this area for shorter distances
  • Kalina University area has good budget-friendly food options for students
  • SV Road is often less congested than the highway for north-south travel
  • Bandra-Kurla Complex (BKC) is easily accessible from Santacruz East
